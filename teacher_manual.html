1. CURRICULAR ALIGNMENT & LESSON SCHEME

This curriculum is aligned with the Inquiry-Based Science Education (IBSE) pedagogy, specifically mimicking the virtual-physical calibration workflows of the European Union Horizon 2020 SPHERE initiative. It directly maps to secondary physics curriculum segments covering:

  • Thermodynamics: Thermal conduction, convection, radiation, thermal barriers, and specific heat capacity calculations.
  • Mathematical Modeling: Rate equations, Newtonian decay curves, and parametric modeling.
  • IoT & Digital Twins: Real-time sensory inputs mapped onto a digital math engine to create predictive twins.

2. RISK ASSESSMENT & HAZARD MITIGATION

Enforce these standard safety mandates to guarantee zero accident rates during live laboratory sessions:

HAZARD LEVEL MANDATED MITIGATION STEPS
Hot Water Scalding HIGH Do not allow students to use water > 80°C. Teachers should pre-boil and dispense water pre-mixed with cold tap water to lock thermal limits at exactly 80°C.
Melted Ice Slippages MEDIUM Place physical ice baths in high-density plastic trays. Provide moisture absorbent towels. Keep ice bath tables completely segregated from laptops and tablets.
Electrical Shock MEDIUM Only permit low-voltage, USB-powered or battery-powered digital sensor readouts. Keep the thermocouple cables long to maximize distance from the computer screen.
Capsule Deformation LOW Ensure thin PET food jars are not exposed to boiling 100°C temperatures which causes polymer warping. Enforce the strict 80°C limit.

4. CLASSROOM EVALUATION & GRADING RUBRIC

Use this standardized 100-point rubric to grade student performance:

GRADING CRITERIA POINTS EXCELLENT (100% Points) SATISFACTORY (70% Points) INSUFFICIENT (30% Points)
1. Assembly Precision 20 Pt Capsule sealed completely. PG7 gland tightened perfectly. DS18B20 wire is centered without touching PET walls. Capsule sealed, but slight water leakage inside jar lid. Probe touching side wall. Severe leaks. Wire gland is loose or untightened. Complete water ingress.
2. Telemetry Diligence 20 Pt All 15 data points entered precisely every 60 seconds. Active team tracking. Missed 2-3 data points. Data recorded late or values estimated. Missed major telemetry slots. Incomplete logging.
3. Correlation Score 30 Pt Physical curve matches mathematical simulator curve with > 90% correlation score. Thermal correlation falls between 75% and 89%. Small wrapping air gaps. Correlation score is < 70% due to major physical leaks or simulation error.
